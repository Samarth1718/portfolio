Science of Resolution

Home / Blog

There is a famous Italian saying “let all your miseries be as short as your New Year resolutions.” People often make resolutions at the start of the New Year and end up their resolutions in two to three week, it is because people never try to come out of their comfort zone. We all need to understand that great things never come from our comfort zone. We take many resolutions and break them because we are targeting the superficial element of the resolution and not the root of it. Roots of the matter like ‘anger’,’laziness’,’greed’,’ego’,’pride’,’jealousy’,’selfishness’ and other negative products have accumulated in us. It needs to be vanquished from our system and not be treated.

Expect in science there can never be a vacuum in our system. It has to be filled with either positives or negatives. And once these positives flow in our personality, where vacuum is created by driving out negatives our outlooks get transformed and we not only get transformed but ready to transform people around us and the process must go on. Driving out negatives alone is not enough. Positive qualities must replace the negative ones. Then everyday will be like a new year. We would not need to make to make special resolutions and efforts to pursue the aspects of life, they would become a way with us. We would thus be on auto mode always, doing the right things right way and going the right way.We don’t have to depend on an external new year to be happy, every day is a new day. All days are god given days and they can’t be bad or inauspicious. He gives us only good and nothing bad. So distinguishing good and bad days to take up something good may not be desirable from practical angle. A journal of clinical psychology study found that people who make resolutions are 10 times more likely to change their behavior than those who don’t.

"Lets make our decisions clear...and set a resolution today to be a star of tomorrow."
