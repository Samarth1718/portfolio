The Genius in you

Home / Blog

Anyone who doesn't do their own thinking will have their lives thought out for them by others willing to do the thinking, therefore thinking doesn't create thoughts is what you call destiny. Man is familiar with the thinking process in his/her own daily experiences. The concentration of all mind is positively registered in motion and its intensity and relaxation of intensity are noted in states of motion of very mind which performs the action and reaction of an thinking process. Man has knowledge within thyself. Inspiration will unlock the doors of knowledge. All knowledge being universal exists in nature, genius awaits the individual who listens.

The genius, the super thinker with imagination who brings beauty to the world is inspired man thinking in light.
